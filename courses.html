Available Courses

  • Introduction to Interdisciplinary Research: This course explores the basics of conducting research that spans across various disciplines, with a particular focus on electrical engineering and entrepreneurship.
  • Entrepreneurship in Technology: Learn the principles of entrepreneurship and how to turn innovative technological ideas into sustainable businesses. The course includes startup funding, business planning, and product development.
  • Advanced Robotics Systems: Focuses on the design and control of robotic systems, integrating electrical, mechanical, and computing concepts. Special attention is given to robotics for industrial and consumer applications.
  • Renewable Energy Systems: This course covers the fundamentals of sustainable energy technologies, including solar, wind, and energy storage systems. It emphasizes the entrepreneurial opportunities in this growing field.
  • Artificial Intelligence in Electrical Systems: A hands-on course where students apply artificial intelligence techniques to optimize electrical systems and solve complex problems in power distribution, automation, and more.
